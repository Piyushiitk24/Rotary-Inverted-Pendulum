\chapter{Mathematical Modelling from First Principles}
\label{ch:modelling}

This chapter derives the nonlinear dynamics of the rotary (Furuta) inverted pendulum from first principles. The objective is to obtain an explicit model suitable for:

\begin{itemize}
  \item numerical parameter identification for the custom rig, and
  \item controller design about the upright equilibrium.
\end{itemize}
